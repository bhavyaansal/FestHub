About Our Fest

FestHub is a modern and responsive web platform built to make college fest experiences more organized and engaging. It eliminates confusion by displaying all event information—categories, timings, and registration links—clearly and accessibly.
With its simple interface and integrated registration forms, students can register effortlessly, stay updated on schedules, and even check results after the fest. EventEase aims to create a smooth digital experience for both organizers and participants.
